Happier

"Happier" by Marshmello is a poignant reflection on love and heartbreak. With its catchy beat and heartfelt lyrics, it explores the bittersweet emotions of longing and acceptance. The song acknowledges the desire for a former lover's happiness, even if it means moving on.

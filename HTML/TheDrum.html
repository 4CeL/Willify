The Drum

"The Drum" by Alan Walker, released in 2022, features his signature electronic style with powerful beats and cinematic melodies. The track blends EDM elements with emotive vocals, starting gently and building to a dynamic drop. Acclaimed by fans and critics, it reinforces Walker's status in modern electronic music.

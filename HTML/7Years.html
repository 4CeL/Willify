7 Years

"7 Years" by Lukas Graham, released in 2015, is a reflective pop song that explores themes of growth, life experiences, and aspirations. With its poignant lyrics and soulful vocals, the song recounts the artist's journey from childhood to adulthood, looking ahead to future milestones.
